The Gen Z Sensei & The Art of Letting Go

Posted on April 13, 2023 • 5 min read • Theme: Stay Curious, Not Fearful

Setting

Picture this: It’s 3 PM on a Tuesday. My new Gen Z colleague—let’s call her The Unbothered Oracle—is snacking on seaweed chips while casually telling ChatGPT to "build a website for dummies, but make it cute." Meanwhile, I’m over here with 17 browser tabs open, all variations of:

  • "What is GitHub?"
  • "How to center a div (please, I’m begging you)."
  • "Why does my CSS look like a toddler threw spaghetti at the screen?"

Key Moment

"If a 25-year-old with AirPods can do this... will AI replace my entire department?!"

The Revelation

She demoed the site in 1 hour—the same time it took me to align one PowerPoint text box. Her secret weapon? Treating AI like a personal assistant rather than something to be feared.

"Gen Z isn't 'lazy'—they're efficiency ninjas. The future belongs to those who yell 'Hey AI, do the thing I don't wanna do' without shame."

The Panic

My existential crisis unfolded in three acts:

  • Denial: "This is just a fluke"
  • Anger: "Why didn't I think of that?"
  • Acceptance: "Maybe I should ask for help too"

The Punchline

Turns out, AI just replaces the 'I-don't-know-where-to-start' panic. The Oracle's real superpower? She asked the robot for help instead of suffering in silence.
